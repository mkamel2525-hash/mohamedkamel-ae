The Philosophy

Building with conviction

I'm Mohamed Kamel — entrepreneur based in the UAE, focused on building technology ventures that solve real problems in the MENA region and beyond. I look for ideas at the intersection of underserved markets and emerging technology.

Every idea here started with a question: why hasn't this been built yet?

What I believe

  • Markets matter more than ideas — find the pull, then build
  • Solve for the 80% underserved, not the 20% already catered to
  • Speed of learning beats speed of building
  • The GCC is one of the most exciting startup ecosystems on earth
  • Great ideas die in isolation — share, iterate, build in public
