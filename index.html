Frequently Asked

Clarity Before Commitment

Considered answers to the questions every serious investor asks.

Why work with an advisor rather than a broker?

A broker sells a transaction; an advisor protects your capital across a portfolio. Mohamed's role is strategic — aligning every acquisition with your long-term wealth objectives, not a single sale.

What is the minimum to start investing in Dubai?

Entry-level off-plan opportunities begin from around AED 1M, while a property-backed Golden Visa typically requires AED 2M. Mohamed tailors a strategy to your specific budget and goals.

Can property ownership secure a UAE Golden Visa?

Yes. A qualifying property investment can grant you and your family 10-year renewable residency. Mohamed structures acquisitions to unlock this where it's part of your objective.

Do you advise on both Dubai and Abu Dhabi?

Both. Each emirate offers distinct advantages — Mohamed provides comparative analysis so capital is allocated with intent across the markets that best fit your strategy.

How does ongoing portfolio management work?

Beyond acquisition, Mohamed monitors yield, market timing, and diversification — advising on when to hold, refinance, or exit, so your portfolio compounds value over time.
